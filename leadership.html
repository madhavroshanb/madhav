MUNoV: Building a more connected world

As CFO of an 8-person team of the Model United Nations of Vienna (MUNoV) 2025, we built the biggest MUN conference in Austria. The event generated Euro 40,000 in revenue, with a large sum earmarked for charity.

It was an extraordinary experience. We established lifelong relationships, debated issues passionately, and discussed how we could make the world a more peaceful, equitable place. I was honored to chair the Historic Security Council Session.
